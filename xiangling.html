About Xiangling:

Xiangling is a chef at Wanmin Restaurant and she is from Liyue.

  1. Xiangling is 4-star character.
  2. She is a polearm/spear user.
  3. Her element in the game is Pryo which is basically fire.

Why Xiangling is one of my favorite characters

  1. Xiangling is cute.
  2. Xiangling's teams are fun to play since they are quickswap-ish.
  3. Xiangling is one of the best damage dealers in the game.
