About Ayaka:

Daughter of the Yashiro Commission's Kamisato Clan from Inazuma. Dignified and elegant, wise and determined. Sincere and pleasant to others. Universally loved by the Inazuma people, she has earned the title of Shirasagi Himegimi.

-description from the official website

  • Ayaka is 5-star character
  • She is a sword user.
  • Her element in the game is Cryo (Ice).

Why Ayaka is one of my favorite characters

  1. Ayaka is very pretty
  2. She is one of the strongest damage dealers in the game.
  3. Her gameplay is very unique and very fun to play since her dash or sprint is different from almost everyone else in the game.
  4. She works well with another favorite character of mine, Kazuha, in a team composition.
